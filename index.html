About Us

Sankarakripa Foundation is a charitable organization dedicated to promoting spiritual values, supporting education, and uplifting communities through service. Rooted in the teachings of Sri Adi Shankaracharya, our mission is to serve selflessly.

Our Services

  • Annadanam (food donation)
  • Veda Parayana support
  • Educational support for students
  • Support for elderly and differently-abled individuals
